The average screen time of teengers

Children in this generation are constantly glued to their screens which include smartphones,tablets,gaming consoles, TVs and computers. On average children who are the ages between 8 and 12 are spending 4-6 hours a day on screens, and teens can be spending up to 9 hours a day on screens.
